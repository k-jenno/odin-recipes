Lasagne

This is the classic lasagne alla Bolognese recipe from the Emilia region in Northern Italy. The Bolognese

sauce is made with a mixture of beef and pork mince. The addition of prosciutto, red wine, cinnamon, and

nutmeg make it truly authentic

Ingredients

  • 500g lean mince (your choice of meat)
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 1/2 diced Onion
  • 1/2 cup diced Capsicum
  • 1/2 cup grated Zucinni
  • 500g Napoltan Sauce (or red sauce of your choosing)
  • 500g Three Cheese Sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Cheese
  • 300g dry lasagne sheets

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 180 degrees Celcius
  2. Heat oil and add Onion and capsicum till cooked
  3. Add Mince meat and cook until browned
  4. Add Carrot and Zucinni, stir for 2 minutes
  5. Stir in Napolitana Sauce until everything is covered
  6. Reduce to simmer and cover for 10 minutes stiring occasionally
  7. In a baking tray Layer cooked sauce then Lasagne sheets then Three Cheese sauce
  8. Repeat above line until out of sauce
  9. Cover top with Greated Cheese
  10. Put in Oven for 45 minutes
  11. Enjoy
