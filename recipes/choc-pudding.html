Chocolate Pudding

Easy-to-make homemade chocolate pudding to satisfy any sweet tooth

Ingredients

  • 3 cups whole milk, divided
  • 1/4 cup Cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup White Sugar
  • 1 cup semisweet Chocolate Chips
  • 1 pinch of Salt

Steps

  1. Combine 1/2 milk and cornstarch in bowl and whisk until smooth
  2. Combine remaining milk with sugar in a medium saucepan over low heat
  3. Slowly whisk in cornstarch mixutre
  4. Cook, whisking as needed to prevent lumps, until mix begins to thicken
  5. Add chocolate chips and salt
  6. Continue stiring until chips are melted and mix is smooth and thick - aprox 7 minutes
  7. Pour cooked mixture into 1 large bowl or 6 smaller bowls/cups
  8. Place platic wrap directly on top of pudding to prevent a skin from forming
  9. Refrigerate for 3 to 4 hours before serving
  10. Enjoy
