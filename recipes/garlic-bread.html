Garlic Bread

Wonderful garlic bread. Quick and easy. Be careful when unwrapping bread,

it will be hot and steamy. Adjust garlic amount to taste.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup butter, softened
  • 3 tablespoons dried minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried Basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 Loaf French Bread, halved lengthwise

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven 200 degrees Celcius
  2. Beat butter, garlic, basil and salt together until smooth
  3. Spread butter mixture onto each half of bread
  4. Wrap each half in aluminum foil and place on baking sheet
  5. Bake in oven for 20 minutes or until golden and crusty
  6. Enjoy
